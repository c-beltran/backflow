Services

Backflow Device Testing

Our area, Nassau County in Long Island, NY, demands testing on a yearly basis to ensure water is safe to consume. As a certified NYS backflow tester, I will ensure that your device is tested properly and submit all paperwork required by the county. Additionally, records of your test are safely kept in case you need a copy in the future.

Water Bacteria Test

How clean and safe is your drinking water? Although, public water is tested to ensure there is no coliform present, this does not guarantee that throught the process of serving your water there are contaminats that flow into your drinking water. I can help you find out if your drinking water contains coliform bacteria such as E. coli.
